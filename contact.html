Frequently Asked Questions

How do I propose a strategic partnership?

expand_more
Interested partners can submit a formal proposal via our inquiry form under the "Global Partnership" category. Our corporate development team reviews all submissions to evaluate alignment with our core strategic pillars and long-term sustainability goals.

What industries does DCO Group currently prioritize?

expand_more
Currently, DCO Group focuses on high-impact sectors including sustainable infrastructure, digital transformation, and specialized manufacturing. We are constantly exploring emerging markets that offer opportunities for operational synergy and technological innovation.

Can I visit your regional offices?

expand_more
Our regional offices serve as hubs for executive operations. While we welcome strategic partners, visits are strictly by appointment only. Please contact the respective regional director through the provided channels to coordinate a visit.

Are you currently accepting media inquiries?

expand_more
Yes, we maintain an active media relations department. For press kits, interview requests, or official statements, please select "General Media Inquiry" in our contact form or reach out to our communications lead directly at our Athens headquarters.
